Nashville's longest running haunt

EST. 1986

Now in it’s 34th season of Haunting Nashville.
In 2014, new Owners Stacey and Ben Dixon of Full Moon Inc. moved the legendary haunt to 3445 Lebanon Pike in Hermitage.
Finding it’s new home in the old Courtyard Cinema.
You will explore the haunt traveling through five of the old theaters.
SLAUGHTERHOUSE winds you on a self guided tour through 5 theaters of the old Courtyard Theater that once set abandoned for 13 years. One Haunted Attraction All theaters for one price. There are No Refunds if you get scared in line you can hold on to your ticket for another try later.The walk through time is about 45 min.

The Haunt

Full Moon Slaughterhouse. Nashville's longest running Haunted Attraction at it's new home and new ownership at 3445 Lebanon Pike Hermitage, TN. 37076. Located just minutes off I-40 at Old Hickory Blvd and Central Pike, in the old Courtyard Theater.

• Tickets are $25.

• Kids 12 and under $15.

Tickets available at the door and online at

www.fullmoonslaughterhouse.com



Dinner and a Movie

The Full Moon Cineplex is home to Nashville's classic Dinner and a Movie theatre. Dinner and Movie $24. General Admission tickets are $7 for movies. If you are wanting dinner please order your tickets before 2pm on Friday or Saturday so we know how many to prepare for.

We have PBR, Miller Lite, Stella, Dos Eques and Sweetwater IPA and 420, White Claws. We also have Blue Moon, Angry Orchard and Catawba White Zombie on Draft.

• Tickets are $25

• Kids 12 and under $15


You can also purchase tickets for Dinner and a Movie and Haunted House movies and options available online at

www.fullmooncineplex.com


Ink and Body Art

Lone Wolf Tattoo won the first ever category for the Best of Nashville Scene in 1999 and was voted Best Tattoo Studio for the next eleven years in a row. Lone Wolf Tattoo has also won multiple years for the Best of Nashville Toast of Music City.

www.lonewolfbodyart.net
